    Frequently Asked Questions

    Find answers to common questions about our window cleaning services.

    What areas do you serve?

    We provide Lawn care service services throughout Navi Mumbai, Maharashtra. For service areas outside these cities, please contact us to discuss availability.

    What is your booking availability?

    We offer online booking from Available Daily Local Time daily. Our standard operating days are Daily. Use our online booking system to schedule at your convenience or call +91 99303 37725.

    How should I prepare for my appointment?

    No special preparation is needed! Simply ensure all windows are accessible and clear of large obstacles. If you have specific instructions or concerns, mention them when booking. We'll handle the rest, including setup and cleanup.

    Are you licensed and insured?

    Yes! Shraddha Nursery is fully licensed and fully insured. We take safety seriously and maintain comprehensive liability coverage for all our work. Your home and property are protected with us.

    Do you offer regular maintenance plans?

    Absolutely! We offer flexible maintenance contracts for both residential and commercial clients. Regular cleaning keeps your windows spotless year-round. Bulk maintenance discounts are available. Contact us to discuss a customized plan for your property.

    What eco-friendly practices do you use?

    We use environmentally safe cleaning solutions that are effective on all window types. Our methods minimize water waste and avoid harsh chemicals that could harm your landscaping or the environment.

    Can you clean high-rise and hard-to-reach windows?

    Yes! We have specialized equipment and trained professionals for high-rise cleaning, skylights, and other hard-to-reach areas. Safety is our top priority with all high-altitude work. Contact us for a consultation on your project.

    What payment methods do you accept?

    We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and digital payments through our secure online booking system. Payment options are flexible and may include deposit arrangements for larger projects.
