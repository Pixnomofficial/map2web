    Frequently Asked Questions

    Do you handle building permits?

    Yes! We handle the entire permitting and zoning process. We draft plans, submit them to the Chicago building department, pay fees, and coordinate all inspections.

    How long does a kitchen remodel take?

    A standard high-end kitchen remodel typically takes between 4 to 6 weeks once construction begins, depending on plumbing and wall modifications.

    Are you licensed and insured?

    Yes, we hold a current Unlimited General Contractor license, carry $5 million in general liability insurance, and provide full workers' compensation coverage.

    Can we stay in our home during an addition?

    For most minor additions, you can remain in the home. For major second-story additions or extensive structural work, we recommend temporary lodging for safety.

    Do you manage subcontractors?

    Yes, completely. We hire, schedule, and supervise all licensed subs — plumbing, electrical, HVAC, framing, and finishing — so you have one accountable point of contact.

    How do you handle project budgets?

    We provide detailed cost breakdowns before any work begins. Change orders are never executed without your written approval, so there are never surprise bills.

    Are you licensed and bonded?

    Yes. We carry a state general contractor license, $2M general liability, workers' compensation, and a performance bond on every project over $25,000.
