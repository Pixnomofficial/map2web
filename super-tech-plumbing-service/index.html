    Frequently Asked Questions

    What is a plumbing emergency?

    Any plumbing issue that threatens active property damage, has a sewer backup, has a completely burst pipe, or leaves you with no running water is an emergency. Call us immediately!

    How long does a standard drain cleaning take?

    Most simple kitchen or bathroom drain cleanings are cleared in 30 to 60 minutes. Deep main line blockages or tree root issues can take 1 to 3 hours.

    Do you offer trenchless sewer repair?

    Yes! Trenchless pipe lining (CIPP) and pipe bursting allow us to repair or replace underground sewer lines without digging up your beautiful lawn, driveway, or walkways.

    Why is my water bill suddenly so high?

    You likely have a hidden leak. The most common causes are running toilets, leaking water heater valves, or an underground slab leak. Call us for a leak inspection today.

    Do you offer emergency plumbing 24/7?

    Yes — our emergency line is staffed 24/7, 365 days a year. Weekend and after-hours rates are disclosed upfront before any work begins.

    How do I know if I have a slab leak?

    Warning signs include wet spots on floors, unexplained increases in water bills, warm spots on slab flooring, or the sound of running water when all fixtures are off.

    Should I repair or replace my water heater?

    If your water heater is under 8 years old, repair is usually cost-effective. Units over 10 years old, or those with rust and corrosion, are typically better replaced with a newer high-efficiency model.
