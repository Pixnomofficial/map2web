    Our Core Services

    System Repairs

    Restore flawless operation to your sprinkler system with our expert irrigation repair and troubleshooting services. We quickly locate broken underground pipes, replace shattered spray heads, repair sticking electric solenoid valves, and trace damaged low-voltage wiring harnesses.

    Spring Activation

    Prepare your lawn for a beautiful growing season with our comprehensive spring sprinkler activation and tuning services. Our technicians slowly charge the mainline, inspect for freeze damage, adjust every spray nozzle for perfect coverage, and program your smart controller.

    Winterization

    Protect your valuable underground pipes, valves, and backflow preventers from catastrophic freeze damage with our guaranteed winterization blowout services. We hook up heavy-duty commercial air compressors to thoroughly evacuate 100% of residual water from your entire system.

    Custom Install

    Invest in a beautiful, maintenance-free landscape with our custom sprinkler system design and installation services. We utilize trenchless vibratory plow machinery to pull heavy-duty poly tubing under your lawn without trenching, installing premium commercial-grade heads and smart controllers.

    24/7 Emergency

    Stop active underground geysers and severe mainline leaks immediately with our priority emergency irrigation repair services. We dispatch technicians quickly to shut off municipal water ties, excavate damaged manifolds, repair broken PVC lines, and restore system integrity.

    Expert Consulting

    Optimize your landscape watering efficiency through our complimentary on-site irrigation system audits and consultations. Our certified designers evaluate precipitation rates, check head-to-head overlap, demonstrate smart controller savings, and provide transparent price quotes.

    Micro-Drip Irrigation Systems

    Conserve water and promote deep root growth with our specialized micro-drip irrigation design and installation services. We install pressure-compensating drip tubing and targeted emitters beneath your mulch beds to hydrate delicate flowers, shrubs, and vegetable gardens perfectly.

    Irrigation Pump & Well Tie-ins

    Maximize your system's water pressure with our professional irrigation booster pump and well tie-in installation services. We install high-performance centrifugal lake intake pumps, submersible well relays, and automated pump start wiring circuits for flawless operation.

    Weather-Based Smart Upgrades

    Slash your seasonal water bills by up to 50% with our professional smart irrigation controller and weather sensor upgrades. We install advanced WiFi controllers from Rachio and Hunter Hydrawise that use real-time local weather forecasts to automatically adjust watering schedules.

    French Drains & Yard Drainage

    Solve soggy lawns, pooling surface water, and foundation erosion with our expert exterior yard drainage and French drain installations. We trench and install perforated drainage tile bedded in washed gravel leading to heavy-duty catch basins and pop-up lawn emitters.

    We transform your landscape into a flourishing, green sanctuary by designing and maintaining highly efficient, automated sprinkler systems. Our mission is to eliminate water waste, ensure uniform head-to-head turf coverage, and deliver prompt, hassle-free seasonal service.

    Our certified irrigation designers and licensed backflow technicians bring decades of specialized hydraulic experience to every residential and commercial property.     Frequently Asked Questions

    When should I blow out my sprinklers?

    We recommend scheduling winterization in October before the first hard freeze.

    Why is one zone not working?

    This is usually caused by a faulty solenoid valve or a break in the control wire.

    Can I save water with a smart timer?

    Yes, smart controllers use weather data to reduce water waste by up to 30%.

    Are you licensed and insured?

    Yes, we carry full liability insurance and all necessary local licenses for your protection.

    Do you offer free estimates?

    Absolutely. We provide transparent, no-obligation quotes for all potential projects.

    When should I winterize?

    Usually before the first hard freeze, typically late October or early November in this region.

    Can you upgrade my controller?

    Yes, we install smart WiFi controllers that can save you up to 50% on your water bill.

    Why are brown spots appearing on my lawn even though my sprinklers run?

    Brown spots are typically caused by sunken heads, clogged nozzles, or improper head spacing leading to poor head-to-head water overlap. We perform full coverage audits to fix this!

    How much water can a smart irrigation controller save?

    Smart controllers utilize real-time local weather forecasts to automatically adjust watering run times, saving homeowners between 30% and 50% on their seasonal water bills.

    What is a backflow preventer and why does it need annual testing?

    A backflow preventer stops lawn chemicals, fertilizers, and stagnant water from flowing backward into your clean drinking water supply. Annual testing is required by municipal health codes to ensure the internal valves hold tight.

    Can you add a new watering zone to my existing sprinkler system?

    Yes! We locate your existing mainline, install new electric solenoid valves, pull new poly tubing, and wire the extra zone into your existing controller.

    What happens if I skip winterization blowout?

    Water left inside underground pipes, valves, and backflow preventers expands when freezing, causing cracked manifolds, shattered PVC pipes, and costly structural damage.
