    Frequently Asked Questions

    How long does hardwood refinishing take?

    A standard residential hardwood sanding and refinishing project takes between 3 to 4 days, depending on the number of coats and humidity levels.

    What is the difference between solid and engineered wood?

    Solid hardwood is a single piece of wood, while engineered wood is made of layered wood slices topped with a natural wood veneer. Engineered wood handles humidity fluctuations better.

    Is luxury vinyl plank suitable for kitchens?

    Yes! Luxury Vinyl Plank (LVP) is 100% waterproof, making it the ideal durable flooring choice for moisture-prone kitchens, bathrooms, and basements.

    How do I eliminate squeaks in my floor?

    Squeaks are caused by floorboards rubbing against nails or joists. We eliminate squeaks during installations by securing the subfloor down with heavy-duty structural screws.

    What is the most durable flooring for pets and kids?

    Luxury Vinyl Plank (LVP) is our top recommendation — 100% waterproof, scratch-resistant, and available in stunning wood-look styles.

    Do you remove old flooring?

    Yes. We offer full tear-out and disposal of old carpet, tile, and hardwood. Disposal fees are included in our upfront quotes.

    How long does flooring installation take?

    An average 3-bedroom home takes 2–3 days. We work around your schedule and protect furniture with drop cloths throughout the process.
